The Georg Simon Ohm School in Cologne is a vocational college, a secondary level II school. In recent years, it has consistently had between 2,200 and 2,500 students, taught by 80 teachers. For the media city of Cologne and the associated businesses in television and IT, it is a recognized dual training partner.
